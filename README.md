**BAUST TEA BAR** is a secure and centralized **food and tea ordering system** developed exclusively for BAUST faculty and authority.

It digitizes the internal ordering process, allowing users to efficiently place requests for tea, snacks, and food items through a simple and structured interface.

**Keywords:** BAUST TEA BAR, food ordering system, faculty portal, institutional cafeteria system, digital ordering platform.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/73097560/115834477-dbab4500-a447-11eb-908a-139a6edaec5c.gif" width="100%">

<!-- Introduction Banner -->
<img src="https://capsule-render.vercel.app/api?type=waving&text=📖%20Introduction&fontSize=32&width=1200&height=130&color=0:9D50BB,100:6E48AA" width="100%">

Traditional food ordering systems in institutional environments are often slow and manual.

**BAUST TEA BAR** solves this by providing:

- 🍽️ Centralized ordering system  
- 🔐 Restricted faculty & authority access  
- ⚡ Fast and efficient service flow  
- 📱 Simple and modern UI  

It improves communication between users and food service management.
